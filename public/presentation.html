⚠️ Current Challenges & Gaps

  • Manual process: Guests call front desk for every query — room availability, check-in time, amenities
  • Peak hour bottleneck: Wait times spike during check-in/check-out hours
  • Inconsistent service: Quality varies by staff member and shift
  • No self-service booking: Multi-step phone/email process for reservations
  • Technology gap: No conversational AI layer for guest interactions
  • Productivity drain: Staff spend 60% of time on repetitive FAQ answers
